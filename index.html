About the Materials and Process

We create innovative toys that inspire learning and play while protecting the planet. Every toy is crafted from sustainable materials like rice, coffee, and ragi husks, transforming agricultural byproducts into beautiful, child-safe learning tools.

  • 100% biodegradable and safe for children
  • Designed with embedded learning technology
  • Sourced from renewable, locally available materials

Rice Husk

Rice husks are durable and naturally resistant to moisture, making them perfect for long-lasting toys.

Coffee Husk

Coffee husks give our toys a unique texture and color, plus they reduce agricultural waste.

Ragi Husk

Ragi husks are lightweight and strong, adding structure while keeping toys eco-friendly.
